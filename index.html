About Cat Haven

At Cat Haven, we provide shelter, love, and care to cats in need, helping them find their forever homes. With a commitment to animal welfare, we ensure every cat receives the attention they deserve before meeting their future family. Join us in our mission to give every cat a loving home.


Founded in 2024, Cat Haven has become a trusted sanctuary for cats of all ages, breeds, and backgrounds. Whether a kitten born on the streets or a senior cat in need of medical care, we strive to create a safe and comfortable environment for every feline friend.
